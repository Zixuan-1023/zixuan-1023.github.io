Writing samples

  • Literature Review
    This literature review surveys recent work in symbolic and audio-based music generation, with a focus on how different modeling paradigms represent musical structure across time. I analyze limitations of purely symbolic representations in capturing long-range temporal coherence, and discuss emerging approaches that integrate continuous acoustic information into sequence modeling. This work reflects my research interests in statistical modeling of musical structure, hierarchical temporal organization, and human-aligned generative systems.

  • NOISE-ROBUST INSTRUMENT CLASSIFICATION UNDER REAL-WORLD SNR CONDITIONS
    This project report presents an experimental study on musical instrument classification under extreme noise conditions. Using CLAP-based embeddings, I investigate robustness strategies including curriculum learning across SNR levels and an adaptive ensemble approach. I propose a harmonic-guided fallback mechanism that introduces structural priors when learned embeddings become unreliable. This work demonstrates my experience with experimental design, failure analysis, and representation choices in audio machine learning.

  • üéπ Monophonic MIDI Synthesizer (Max/MSP)
    An interactive and visualized monophonic MIDI synthesizer built in Max/MSP, designed as a playable instrument that supports real-time performance through both a virtual on-screen keyboard and external MIDI controllers. The synthesizer integrates waveform selection, ADSR envelope shaping, LFO-based modulation (frequency and depth), nonlinear overdrive, filtering, and time-based effects, with each processing module individually controllable and bypassable.

  • üé® Audio Visualizer JUCE Plugin
    Audio Visualizer is a real-time audiovisual plugin built with JUCE, translating live audio into an expressive, physically inspired particle system inside a DAW. The plugin runs entirely in real time, analyzing incoming audio on the audio thread and mapping continuous control signals to a custom visual engine that reacts to musical energy and timbral change. Its particle-orbit visual model drives motion, scale, color, and rotation through smoothed audio parameters. An optional Emotion mode uses learned latent controls to shape higher-level visual behaviors, allowing the visualization to feel responsive rather than purely reactive.
